ABOUT US

Atharva Group of Institutes is a registered society, which was established in the year 1998 under the dynamic leadership of Hon. Shri Sunil Ji Rane Sir. The trust has the objective of imparting excellence in education to the frontiers of learning and education in Mumbai. AGI's Educational Complex is set up on a gigantic and spacious campus of 6 acres situated in the heart of western suburbs of Malad with convenient access by bus /train. AGI's Complex is the perfect setting of preofessional environment with a friendly touch to the scene.

How do I register for Rhythm Ember?

Can I do offline registration for events?

Is the entry free?


The entry has been and will always be free for all college students with valid ID card of college or school.

What are the nearest railway and metro stations to reach Atharva College of Engineering?


The AGI campus is located in Malad, around 3 km from the Malad Railway Station, around 4 km from Kandivali Railway Station and around 0.5 km from Valnai(Mith Chowky) Metro Station.The Campus is well connected by road to each of these three major arrival points. As such, you can reach easily by bus or readily hire a taxi or an auto-rickshaw from any of these to reach the Campus.
