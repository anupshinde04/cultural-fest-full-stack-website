Foot Snooker

REG. FEES: INR 100
PRIZES WORTH: INR 2,000

ABOUT

A table sport, which mimics pool, but is played in a manner in which players stand on the table and kick the cue ball to pocket colored balls, rather than striking it with a cue stick.


REGISTER

RULES

1. It is an Individual Event.
2. Player has to pot 3 balls before his opponent wins. (2 stripes or 2 solids and 1 black ball).
3. The black ball must be potted last.
4. Time limit for a game will be 5 mins. In case the time is up the one who has potted the most balls wins.
5. In case of a tie toss will decide the winner.
6. Event head's decision will be considered final.
